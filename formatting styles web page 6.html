HTML FORMATTING STYLES

HTML formatting styles are defined with the style name < > tag.

HERE ALL THE FORMATTING STYLES HAVE BEEN APPLIED TO ONLY WORD- KHUSHI CHADHA , FOR EASY DIFFERENTIABILITY BETWEEN WORDS.

1. BOLD---> Is used to bold the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

1. STRONG---> Is used to bold the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

2. ITALIC ---> Is used to change the style of the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

3. MARKED ---> Is used to highlight the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

4. SMALLER ---> Is used to reduce the size of the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

5. UNDERLINE ---> Is used to underline the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

6. STRIKE ---> Is used to strike the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

7. EMPHASIZE ---> Is used to emphasize the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

8. DELETED ---> Is used to delete the text.Similar to that of strike .
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

9. INSERTED ---> Is used to insert the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

10. BIGGER ---> Is used to largen the text.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

11. SUBSCRIPT ---> Is used to lower the text in compare to other.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA

12. SUPERSCRIPT ---> Is used to upper the text in compare to other.
For instance ---> HEYY, I AM KHUSHI CHADHA
